Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Mariana Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Bring Water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
    1. Add salt to taste.
    2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add mariana sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
  4. Mix cooked spaghetti with mariana sauce.
  5. Enjoy!
